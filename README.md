## Quick Start Guide

### CUSTODY SCHEDULE BUILDER — USER-FRIENDLY QUICK CARD

---

**OPEN THE PAGE**

| Schedule | Link |
|---|---|
| School Year | https://custody-schedule.onrender.com/school-year.html |
| Summer | https://custody-schedule.onrender.com/ |

You can switch between them using the menu at the top.

---

**PICK A STARTING PLAN**

Choose a plan that fills in the calendar for you.

- **School Year choices:** Option 1, Option 2, Week-On/Week-Off, or Blank
- **Summer choices:** 1-week blocks, 2-week blocks, or choose your own block size

---

**CHANGE A DAY**

Tap or click a day to switch who has it.
It goes in this order: **Robin → Isaac → Unassigned → back to Robin.**

- On a computer: click and drag one day onto another to swap them
- On a phone or tablet: press and hold a day until it dims, then slide it to another day

---

**LOCK A DAY**

If you want a day to stay the same, lock it.
Right-click (computer) or press and hold (phone/tablet), then choose **"Lock."**
Locked days cannot be changed by accident.

---

**ADD A NOTE**

You can write a small note on any day.
Right-click or press and hold, then choose **"Add Note."**
Examples: Camp, Travel, Holiday, Grandma's House.

---

**CHECK THE TOTALS**

At the top you can see:

| | |
|---|---|
| Robin | how many days Robin has |
| Isaac | how many days Isaac has |
| Total Assigned | how many days are filled |
| Split | the percentage between them |

If you want the full 365-day total, keep both pages open at the same time.

---

**USE THE BUTTONS**

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| Clear All | Erase everything (locked days stay) |
| Reset to Template | Go back to the starting plan but keep locked days |
| Export Schedule | Shows a text version you can copy and save |

---

**COLORS**

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟦 Blue | Robin |
| 🟧 Orange | Isaac |
| ⬜ Grey | Unassigned |
| 🔒 Dark grey | Locked |
| Gold border | School break |
| Green border | No-school day |

---

**IF SOMETHING LOOKS WEIRD**

- **Reset to Template** to fix the pattern
- **Clear All** to start over
- If dragging doesn't work on a phone, press and hold a little longer

## Detailed User Guide

**North Bend School District · 3rd Grade · 2025–2026**

These tools let you interactively build, compare, and export custody schedules for mediation.
No login required. Works in any web browser on a computer, phone, or tablet.

---

### 🔗 Quick Links

| Tool | URL |
|---|---|
| ☀ Summer 2026 Schedule | https://custody-schedule.onrender.com/ |
| 📚 School Year 2025–26 Schedule | https://custody-schedule.onrender.com/school-year.html |

Use the navigation bar at the top of each page to switch between the two tools.

---

### Step 1 — Choose a Template

Click one of the **Template** buttons at the top of the page to pre-fill the calendar with a starting schedule:

**School Year page:**
| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| Option 1 — Robin-Preferred | Sun/Mon/Tue/Thu = Robin · Wed/Fri/Sat = Isaac |
| Option 2 — Alternative Pattern | Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri = Robin · Sun/Wed/Sat = Isaac |
| Isaac's Proposal — Week On/Off | Full weeks alternate: Robin's week, then Isaac's week (approx. 50/50 split) |
| Blank / Unassigned | Clears everything so you can build from scratch |

**Summer page:**
| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| Version A — 1-Week Alternating | Alternates by week, 7-day blocks |
| Version B — 2-Week Alternating | Alternates by 2-week blocks |
| Version C — Custom Block Length | You choose how many days per block |

---

### Step 2 — Adjust Individual Days

You have three ways to change any day:

#### Click a day
Cycles through: **Robin → Isaac → Unassigned → Robin...**
Each click instantly updates the totals.

#### Drag a day (desktop)
- Click and hold a day block
- Drag it onto another day
- Release — the two days **swap assignments**

#### Drag a day (phone or tablet)
- Press and hold a day block until it dims slightly
- Slide your finger to another day
- Lift your finger — the two days swap

#### Right-click a day (desktop) / Long-press (phone)
Opens an options panel where you can:
- **Lock / Unlock** a day — locked days cannot be moved or reassigned accidentally (shown with a 🔒 icon)
- **Add a note** — e.g. "Camp", "Travel", "Holiday" (appears as small italic text on the day)

---

### Step 3 — Read the Totals

At the top of each page you'll see:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Robin | Days assigned to Robin on this schedule |
| Isaac | Days assigned to Isaac on this schedule |
| Total Assigned | How many days have been assigned so far |
| Split / Balance | The percentage or balance status for this schedule |

#### Full Year Summary (blue-grey section)
Shows the **combined totals across both schedules** (summer + school year = 365 days).
- To see accurate full-year numbers, **open both pages** in the same browser session
- The split percentage updates automatically every time you make a change

---

### Actions

| Button | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Clear All** | Removes all assignments on this page (locked days are kept) |
| **Reset to Template** | Restores the current template's pattern, keeping any locked days as-is |
| **Export Schedule** | Opens a text summary of the full schedule you can copy and paste |

## Tips for Mediation Sessions

- **Start with a template** to establish a baseline, then adjust individual days as needed
- **Use Isaac's Proposal** to instantly see what a true 50/50 week-on/week-off split looks like
- **Switch templates** at any time — the calendar updates instantly so you can compare options side by side (just remember switching resets unadjusted days)
- **Lock key days** before switching templates if you've agreed on specific days and don't want them changed
- **Export Schedule** generates a clean plain-text summary suitable for pasting into an email or document
- The **Full Year Summary** shows the real impact of each schedule choice across all 365 days

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Will my changes be saved if I close the browser?**
A: No — changes are not saved between sessions. The tool is designed for live use during mediation. Use **Export Schedule** to save a copy before closing.

**Q: Can two people use it at the same time?**
A: Each person sees their own version in their own browser. Changes one person makes do not affect the other person's screen.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: Click **Reset to Template** to start over from the template, or **Clear All** to clear everything and start from scratch.

**Q: The drag-and-drop isn't working on my phone.**
A: Press and hold the day block for about half a second before sliding — it needs a brief hold to start the drag.
